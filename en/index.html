Health Without Border

Charitable organization. London.

About Us

HWB – is an organization, with the main aim to provide financial support to medical institutions and patients, who need medical help in Europe.

Our Goal

The overall mission of our organization is to support the development of medicine in all European countries. We are aimed at achieving a higher level of provision standard in hospitals and accessibility of qualitative health care of patients from all over the world, both in private and in public health institutions around Europe.

The Team

HWB – is a team of skillful international professionals. Since we offer a multiskilled practice of service, our team includes patients supervising experts , financial professionals, lawyers, medical representatives, physicians, regional managers and interpreters.

Charity Funds

To raise funds, “Health Without Border” creates special volunteer programs, as well as organizes and takes part in social oriented programmes of European countries for the purpose of charity granting and receiving donations from organizations and individuals.

Activities

HWB - “Health Without Border" - has a wide range of activities, such as :

Charity.

Primarily, “Health Without Border” is an organization that assists to obtain qualitative health care in the best medical institutions in Europe, as well as to provide financial support for patients who are unable to pay for their treatment. The applicants are those who urgently need emergency surgery in oncology, cardiology surgery, transplanting internals and bone -marrow or stem cells transplantation. More information on patients care you will find on the page of our blog.

Transportation of “imported organs”

HWB - “Health Without Border” accords assistance in the transportation organization of “imported organs”, bone-marrow and stem cells around the European territory. To provide more information, please fill in the primary form "Transportation of “imported organs” in section "Contacts".


Assistance to medical institutions.

In the program «Assistance to medical institutions" HWB Organization provides help for medical institutions in Europe in buying and transporting expensive equipment .

Social activity.

The Organization “Health Without Border” uses opportunities to work with public international organizations, and is also a frequent participant in governmental programs of European countries and medical organizations associations. This allows, time efficiently, to increase the possibility of financial support organization for patients ‘ treatment and selection of the best medical facilities for the patients. If you want to help us, to become a volunteer or to support our organization, please fill in a special form in the "Contact Us " sector.
